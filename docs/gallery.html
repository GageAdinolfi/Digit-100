3D Modeling and Spatial Annotation Project

In this group project we had to create a 3d scan of a part of the library and annotate different objects in it.

Me and my group member decided to scan a part of the library that had a series of desks, and was surrounded by bookshelves.

For our annotations we went with a spooky theme and annotated things such as a body without a head, a floating torso, and an abandoned study area.

We also included factual annotations such as book divider or the history of how libraries are organized over time.
